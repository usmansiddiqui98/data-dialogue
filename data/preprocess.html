static clean_sentence(sentence, stop_words)

Clean a single string by removing tags, resolving contractions, removing digits and special characters, tokenizing, changing to lower case and removing punctuations, removing stop words, finding the POS tag for each token, and lemmatizing each token.

Parameters:

  • stop_words (list) – A list of stop words to be removed from the string.
